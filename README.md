# Task 1 - Digital Literacy Awareness Infographic
## Overview
As part of **CSE0001 - Digital Literacy**, this task required creating a one-page infographic to help batchmates understand the meaning and importance of digital literacy. The infographic highlights key aspects of navigating the digital world safely and professionally.
## Tool Used
I used **Canva** to design the infographic. Canva provided easy-to-use templates, icons, and layouts that made the design visually appealing and clear.
## Infographic Content
The infographic covers the following topics:
- **What is Digital Literacy?**  
  Ability to use technology effectively, safely, and responsibly.
- **Useful Digital Tools for Students**  
  Google Workspace, Quizlet, Khan Academy, Evernote.
- **Safe Internet Practices**  
  Strong passwords, avoiding scams, updating software, protecting personal info.
- **Email Etiquette**  
  Clear subject lines, concise writing, polite tone, proofreading.
## Reflection
Designing the infographic was interesting because it required condensing complex ideas into short, clear points. The challenge was balancing visual appeal with readability, ensuring the slide remained uncluttered while covering multiple topics.

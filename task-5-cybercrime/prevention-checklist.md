# 🛡️ Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist





##### \- Verify email senders and links before clicking or entering credentials.

##### \- Use strong, unique passwords with a mix of letters, numbers, and symbols.

##### \- Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) on all important accounts.

##### \- Avoid oversharing personal details (phone number, address, financial info) on social media.

##### \- Be cautious with attachments — never download files from unknown sources.

##### \- Keep software and antivirus updated to protect against malware.

##### \- Check UPI/online payment requests carefully — confirm the recipient before sending money.

##### \- Never share UPI PIN, OTP, or banking passwords with anyone.

##### \- Report suspicious activity immediately via the National Cyber Crime Portal (cybercrime.gov.in).

##### \- Call the Cyber Crime Helpline (1930) for urgent fraud or online payment issues.
